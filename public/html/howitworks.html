FiftyList - How it Works.

About Allowed Users.

Spotify imposes a limit of 25 free users that must be added by the developer. These users can then use the web application.

When prompted to add your personal Spotify account during the introductory authentication screen, it is to include that email in the 25 trial users provided by Spotify.

You will receive an email once your Spotify account has been added to the allowed users list.

To provide you with a personalized experience, this service will require permissions to access your Spotify account (with your explicit consent only). For more information, review the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

If you would like to grant access to your personal Spotify account or have any other questions or concerns, please contact contact@ivanluna.dev.


Guide on How to Use FiftyList.

Once you authenticate with your Spotify account, please note that for the obtained results to be reflected in all chosen options, the account (whether free or premium) should have significant listening time of more than 6 months.

If you have an account with over 6 months of listening time, the web application will retrieve more comprehensive results and the recommendation engine will be broader.

As indicated in the following instructions, you first need to press the "1. Select Time Period:" button.

Here you will select between one month, six months, or all time.

Then press the "2. Get 50 most listened tracks" button.

This button will display the results directly in the same tab, organizing the top 50 songs listened by you in that time period. At the end, 20 new recommendations based on those first 50 results will be shown.

If you want to navigate to a certain song, artist, or album, simply click on them, here the pointer is placed over the artist name and by clicking on it a new tab (in Spotify) will open with that information. (Applicable also for both song or album)

Then, you can:

  • Generate a Fifty Card: This will generate a static website for you or your contacts to access those obtained results. This functionality also allows sharing to your social networks with a unique link.

  • Revoke access: Provides a step-by-step guide on how to revoke our access to your Spotify account.

Finally, by clicking 'Export to Playlist' (Results and Recommendations), these songs will be added to a new playlist in your Spotify account, as appropriate.
