Revoke Access to Fiftylist


Follow the steps below to revoke our access to Spotify:

1. Open Spotify and log in.
2. Visit the Manage Apps section.
3. Here you can manage the apps that have access to your account.

4. Locate and select "FiftyList - Fetching your top jams."
5. Confirm your action to remove access.
6. This will revoke the access and permissions granted to FiftyList. Access will only be granted to our app again if you choose to authorize it once more.

Finally, as a reminder, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use, if you have any concerns or questions you can contact us at contact@ivanluna.dev.
